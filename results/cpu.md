### Notes

- Prefill numbers have ~10–15% run-to-run variance (compute-bound, OS scheduling jitter); decode is stable within ~2%.
- Llama GGUF prefill in infernum uses the same graph executor for both Q8_0 and Q4_0 but loads quantized weights — the graph itself is F32.
- Qwen has no llama.cpp comparison — graph mode does not yet support GGUF, and the SafeTensors naming convention differs from Llama's.
- F32 GGUF conversion for SmolLM2-360M was skipped (Python `transformers` not installed in this environment).
